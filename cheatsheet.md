# Korean Grammar & Vocabulary Cheatsheet

## Grammar

### 1. Continuous Tense (-고 있다)
Used to express an action currently in progress.
*   **Formation:** Verb Stem + -고 있다
*   **Present Polite:** -고 있어요 (is/am/are ...ing)
*   **Past Polite:** -고 있었어요 (was/were ...ing)
*   *Example:* 지금 공부하고 있어요. (I am studying now.)

### 2. Ability & Possibility (-(으)ㄹ 수 있다/없다)
Used to express "can" or "cannot". `수` literally means "way" or "possibility".
*   **Vowel stem:** -ㄹ 수 있다/없다
*   **Consonant stem:** -을 수 있다/없다
*   *Example:* 갈 수 있어요 (I can go), 먹을 수 없어요 (I cannot eat).

### 3. Causal Connector (-아/어/여서)
Used to link a reason/cause to a result ("Because...", "So...").
*   **Formation:** Conjugate verb to present tense (remove -요) + -서.
*   *Example:* 아파서 (Because it hurts), 없어서 (Because I don't have it).

## Vocabulary Roots (Hanja)

### Home & Living
*   **가 (家 - House/Family):** 가구 (Furniture), 가사 (Housework), 가족 (Family)
*   **실 (室 - Room):** 거실 (Living room), 침실 (Bedroom), 욕실 (Bathroom)
*   **주 (住 - To live / 廚 - Kitchen):** 주택 (Housing), 주반 (Kitchen)

### Human & Body
*   **두 (頭 - Head):** 두통 (Headache), 두뇌 (Brain)
*   **안 (眼 - Eye):** 안과 (Eye clinic), 안경 (Glasses)
*   **심 (心 - Heart/Mind):** 심장 (Heart), 관심 (Interest), 조심 (Caution)
*   **수 (手 - Hand):** 수술 (Surgery), 박수 (Applause)
*   **족 (足 - Foot):** 족발 (Pig's feet dish)

## Time & Logic
*   **후 (後 - After):** 후에 (After/Later)
*   **전 (前 - Before):** 전에 (Before/Ago)
