# Korean Grammar & Vocabulary Cheatsheet

## Grammar

### 1. Continuous Tense (-고 있다)
Used to express an action currently in progress.
*   **Formation:** Verb Stem + -고 있다
*   **Present Polite:** -고 있어요 (is/am/are ...ing)
*   **Past Polite:** -고 있었어요 (was/were ...ing)
*   *Example:* 지금 공부하고 있어요. (I am studying now.)

## Vocabulary Roots (Hanja)

### Home & Living
*   **가 (家 - House/Family):** 가구 (Furniture), 가사 (Housework), 가족 (Family)
*   **실 (室 - Room):** 거실 (Living room), 침실 (Bedroom), 욕실 (Bathroom)
*   **주 (住 - To live / 廚 - Kitchen):** 주택 (Housing), 주방 (Kitchen)

## Time & Logic
*   **후 (後 - After):** 후에 (After/Later)
*   **전 (前 - Before):** 전에 (Before/Ago)
