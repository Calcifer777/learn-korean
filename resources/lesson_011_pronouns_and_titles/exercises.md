# Lesson 011: Pronouns, Possessives & Titles - Exercises

## Round 1 (Hierarchy Check)
1. **Prompt:** My (formal) name is Marco.
   - **User:** 제 일음이 마르코 혜요
   - **Correction:** 제 **이름은** 마르코**예요**. (Spelling & 이에요/예요 rule)
2. **Prompt:** This thing is a bus stop.
   - **User:** 이것은 정료장이 혜요
   - **Correction:** 이것은 **정류장이에요**.
3. **Prompt:** Does the doctor (you) like movies?
   - **User:** 영화를 의사 손생님이 좋아헤요?
   - **Correction:** **의사 선생님이** 영화를 **좋아해요?** (Avoiding "you")
4. **Prompt:** We (formal) are at the hospital.
   - **User:** 병원에 저희 헤요
   - **Correction:** **저희는** 병원에 **있어요**. (Location vs Definition)
5. **Prompt:** Is that thing near you a shopping mall?
   - **User:** 저것은 쇼핑몰이 헤요?
   - **Correction:** **그것은** 쇼핑몰**이에요?** (Demonstrative proximity)

## Round 2 (Casual vs. Formal)
1. **Prompt:** My (casual) friend is at school.
   - **User:** 네 진구가 학교에 있어요 -> **내 친구가...**
2. **Prompt:** Where are you (older brother) going?
   - **User:** 형은 오디에 가요? -> **어디에**
3. **Prompt:** That thing over there is my (formal) car.
   - **User:** 그것은 제 차에요 -> **저것은 제 차예요**
4. **Prompt:** We (casual) are students.
   - **User:** 우리는 학생이에요 (Correct!)
5. **Prompt:** This is not water.
   - **User:** 이것은 물이 아니에요 (Correct!)

## Round 3 (Scenario Practice)
1. **Boss:** 제(저희) 집이 서울에 있어요. (Correct!)
2. **Friend:** 우리는 영화관에 같이 가요. (Correct!)
3. **Price:** 이것은 얼마예요? (Correct!)
4. **Sister:** 누나는 커피를 좋아해요? (Correct!)
5. **Teacher:** 저는 선생님을 좋아해요. (Correct!)

## Active Writing
1. **Prompt:** This (near me) is my (formal) phone.
   - **User:** 이것은 제 핸드폰이에요 (Perfect!)
2. **Prompt:** Teacher, do you like Kimchi?
   - **User:** 선생님은 김치를 좋아해요? (Perfect!)
3. **Prompt:** That (near you) is our (formal) house.
   - **User:** 그것은 저희 집이에요 (Perfect!)
