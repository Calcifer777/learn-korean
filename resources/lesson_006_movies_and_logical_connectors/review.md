## Grammar Covered
- **Noun Modifiers (Past):** V + -(으)ㄴ (e.g., 본 영화 - the movie I watched)
- **Causal Connector:** -아/어/여서 (Because/So)
  - *Rule:* No past tense in the first clause.
- **Before ...ing:** V + -기 전에
- **About:** N + 에 대해서
