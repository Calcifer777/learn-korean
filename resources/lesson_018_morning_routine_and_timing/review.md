## Vocabulary Learned (from TTMIK Story 12)
- **출근 (To go to work):** 出 (out) + 勤 (work)
- **퇴근 (To leave work):** 退 (retreat) + 勤 (work)
- **오전 (AM):** 午 (noon) + 前 (before)
- **오후 (PM):** 午 (noon) + 後 (after)
- **세수 (Wash face/hands):** 洗 (wash) + 手 (hand)
- **갈아입다:** To change clothes.
- **갈아타다:** To transfer (vehicles).
- **일어나다:** To wake up.
- **아침 / 점심 / 저녁:** Morning / Lunch / Dinner.

## Grammar Covered
- **Immediate Sequence:** V + -자마자 (As soon as).
- **Approximate Time:** N + 쯤 (Around).
- **Adverbs:** -게 (간단하게 - simply).
- **Frequency:** -(으)ㄹ 때도 있다 (There are times when... / Sometimes).
- **Negation (Inability):** 못 / -지 못하다 (Cannot).
