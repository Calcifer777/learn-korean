## Grammar Covered
- **Adjective Modifiers:** A + -(으)ㄴ (e.g., 예쁜, 좋은).
  - *ㅂ irregular:* Drop ㅂ, add -운 (무서운, 매운).
- **Emotional Verbs (-아/어/여하다):** Turns a state into an action that can take an object (-을/를).
- **State Verbs (-되다):** Indicates a state becoming or starting (시작되다).
- **Adverbs (-게):** Review from Lesson 007.
