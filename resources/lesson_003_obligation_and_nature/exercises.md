# Lesson 003: Obligation & Nature - Exercises

## Guided Practice Round 1

1. **Prompt:** Translate: "It is raining, so I must buy an umbrella."
   **Corrected Answer:** 비가 와서 우산을 사야 돼요.

2. **Prompt:** Fill in the blank: "태풍이 와서 집에 __________(must stay/be)."
   **Corrected Answer:** 태풍이 와서 집에 있어야 돼요.

3. **Prompt:** Translate: "The air is bad, so I must clean."
   **Corrected Answer:** 공기가 나빠서 청소해야 돼요.

4. **Prompt:** Fill in the blank: "기온이 높아서 에어컨을 __________(must turn on)."
   **Corrected Answer:** 기온이 높아서 에어컨을 켜야 돼요.

5. **Prompt:** Translate: "Because of the snow, I must walk carefully."
   **Corrected Answer:** 눈이 와서 조심히 걸어야 돼요.

## Guided Practice Round 3

1. **Prompt:** Translate: "The house is small, so I must move."
   **Corrected Answer:** 집이 작아서 이사해야 돼요.

2. **Prompt:** Fill in the blank: "비가 와서 우비를 __________(must wear)."
   **Corrected Answer:** 비가 와서 우비를 입어야 돼요.

3. **Prompt:** Translate: "Because the wind blows, I must wear a hat."
   **Corrected Answer:** 바람이 많이 불어서 모자를 써야 돼요.

## Active Writing

1. **Prompt:** A Stormy Day: Describe what you and your friend have to do because of a typhoon and rain.
   **Corrected Answer:** 비가 와서 우비를 입어야 돼요. 친구는 우산을 써야 돼요.

2. **Prompt:** A Hot Afternoon: Describe what needs to happen in the house when the temperature is high.
   **Corrected Answer:** 오늘 기온이 아주 높아서 에어컨을 켜야 돼요. 그리고 물을 많이 마셔야 돼요.

3. **Prompt:** Nature Protection: Describe what we must do because the air is bad.
   **Corrected Answer:** 공기가 나빠서 마스크를 써야 돼요. 그리고 집을 청소해야 돼요.
