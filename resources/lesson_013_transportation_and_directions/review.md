## Vocabulary Learned (from TTMIK Story 08)

- **정류장 (Bus Stop):** 停 (stop) + 留 (stay) + 場 (place)
- **도착 (Arrival):** 到 (arrive) + 着 (arrive)
- **앞문/뒷문:** Front door / Back door.
- **내리다:** To get off.
- **누르다:** To press (벨을 누르다).
- **걸리다:** To take time (시간이 걸리다).
- **정도:** About/Approximately.
- **중에서:** Among/Out of.

## Grammar Covered
- **Selection:** N + 중에서.
- **Exclusion:** N + 만.
- **Approximation:** N + 정도.
- **Hierarchy:** Special particles (-만, -도, -은/는) replace Case particles (-이/가, -을/를) but stack with Logical particles (-에, -에서, -으로).
