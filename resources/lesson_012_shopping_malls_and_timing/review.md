# Lesson 012 Review

## Performance
- **Score:** 7.5/10
- **Strengths:** Excellent grasp of **-(으)ㄹ 때** (When) logic, including irregulars (더울 때). Strong use of **-(으)ㄹ 수 있다** (Can).
- **Weaknesses:** Spelling consistency (`맛있는`, `한국어`, `날씨`, `오늘`). Minor confusion with verb coordination suffixes (`-거나` vs `-나`).

## Vocabulary Learned (from TTMIK Story 07)
- **쇼핑몰 (Shopping Mall):** Shopping + Mall
- **생기다 (To appear/form):** Used in "새로 생긴" (Newly opened/formed).
- **식사하다 (To have a meal):** 食 (food) + 事 (matter) + 하다
- **수영장 (Swimming pool):** 水 (water) + 泳 (swim) + 場 (place)
- **찜질방 (Sauna):** Korean dry sauna.
- **옷가게 (Clothing store):** 옷 (clothes) + 가게 (shop)
- **기분 (Mood):** 氣 (energy) + 分 (part) -> Feeling/Mood.
- **힘들다:** To be hard/tiring.

## Grammar Covered
- **Possibility:** V + -(으)ㄹ 수 있다/없다.
- **Verb Choice (Or):** V + -거나.
- **Time Clause (When):** V/A + -(으)ㄹ 때.
- **Contrast:** -지만 (Briefly introduced: "Tiring *but* fun").
