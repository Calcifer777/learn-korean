# Lesson 016: The Zoo & Interaction - Exercises

## Round 1 (Translation)
1. **Prompt:** There are many people at the zoo.
   - **User:** 사람들이 동물에서 많아요 -> **동물원에** (Location state)
2. **Prompt:** I give bread to the friend.
   - **User:** 친수한테 빵을 줘요 -> **친구** (Spelling)
3. **Prompt:** Since it is hot, please drink water.
   - **User:** 다운 날씨가 있으니까 -> **날씨가 더우니까** (ㅂ irregular)
4. **Prompt:** It might even rain today.
   - **User:** 비가 올 수도 있어요 (Correct!)
5. **Prompt:** Since it's dangerous, please don't go.
   - **User:** 의하말 있으니까 -> **위험하니까** (Spelling)

## Round 2 (Causal & Imperatives)
1. **Prompt:** Please be careful since it is cold.
   - **User:** 추우니까 위험해세요 -> **조심하세요** (Meaning)
2. **Prompt:** I buy bread for my younger sibling.
   - **User:** 동생한테 빵을 사요 (Correct structure)
3. **Prompt:** The doctor might even be at the hospital.
   - **User:** 의사 산생님이... 있을 수도 있어요 (Correct grammar, spelling: 선생님)
4. **Prompt:** Since there is no food, let's go to the restaurant.
   - **User:** 음식이 않아니까 -> **없으니까** (Negation choice)
5. **Prompt:** Please read books to the children.
   - **User:** 아이들한테 책을 읽어세요 -> **읽으세요/읽어 주세요**

## Round 3 (Advanced Forms)
1. **User:** 오눌 선생님을 만나을 수도 -> **오늘... 만날** (Spelling/Future modifier)
2. **User:** 재미있니까 -> **재미있으니까** (Consonant ending)
3. **User:** 빵을 동물들한테 주지 마세요 (Correct!)
4. **User:** 저는 돈이 없어니까 -> **없으니까**
5. **User:** 서을에 동물원이 일 수도 -> **있을 수도** (Existence)

## Active Production
1. **User:** 동물들을 볼 수 있어요 (Correct!)
2. **User:** 가리가 의한하니까 주심해야 해요 -> **거리(길)가 위험하니까 조심해야**
3. **User:** 친수한테 산물을 줘요 -> **친구... 선물**

## Active Writing
1. **User:** 날씨가 추우니까 옷을 입어요 (Correct!)
2. **User:** 한국아를 말할 수고 없어요 -> **한국어를 말할 수도 있어요/없어요**
3. **User:** 무룰 덩물한테 주세요 -> **물을 동물들한테**
