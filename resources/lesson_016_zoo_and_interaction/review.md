# Lesson 016 Review

## Performance
- **Score:** 8/10
- **Strengths:** Strong grasp of the nuances for `-ㄹ 수도 있다` (might/even) and the plural marker `-들`. Correctly identifying causal relationships with `-(으)니까`.
- **Weaknesses:** Spelling of vocabulary (위험하다, 친구, 동물, 선물). Conjugation of existence/definition verbs (있다 vs 이다) in complex forms.

## Vocabulary Learned (from TTMIK Story 10)
- **동물 (Animal):** 動 (move) + 物 (thing)
- **동물원 (Zoo):** 동물 + 園 (garden)
- **위험하다 (Dangerous):** 危 (danger) + 險 (steep)
- **조심하다 (Careful):** 操 (grasp) + 心 (mind)
- **주다 (To give):** 주다 / 드리다 (honorific).
- **아이 (Child):** 아이들 (Children).
- **거리/길:** Street/Road.

## Grammar Covered
- **Target (Living):** -한테 / -에게.
- **Plural:** -들.
- **Potential:** -ㄹ 수도 있다 (Might even / Can also).
- **Causal (Subjective):** -(으)니까 (Since/Because -> used with commands/suggestions).
- **Benefactive:** -아/어 주다 (Do for someone).
- **Passive (Intro):** 보이다 (To be seen).
