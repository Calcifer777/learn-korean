# Lesson 001: Continuous Tense & Home Life - Exercises

## Guided Practice

1. **Prompt:** Translate: "I am doing housework in the kitchen."
   **Corrected Answer:** 주방에서 가사를 하고 있어요.

2. **Prompt:** Fill in the blank: "아버지는 지금 __________(living room)에서 TV를 보__________ (is watching)."
   **Corrected Answer:** 아버지는 지금 거실에서 TV를 보고 있어요.

3. **Prompt:** Translate: "Where are you living now?" (To live = 살다)
   **Corrected Answer:** 지금 어디에 살고 있어요?

4. **Prompt:** Fill in the blank: "어제 친구가 __________ (bedroom)에서 책을 읽__________ (was reading)."
   **Corrected Answer:** 어제 친구가 침실에서 책을 읽고 있었어요.

5. **Prompt:** Translate: "We are buying furniture for the new house." (New house = 새 집, To buy = 사다)
   **Corrected Answer:** 새 집을 위해 가구를 사고 있어요.

## Active Production

1. **Prompt:** What are you doing in the living room right now?
   **Corrected Answer:** 지금 거실에서 쉬고 있어요.

2. **Prompt:** What was your family doing in the kitchen this morning?
   **Corrected Answer:** 아침에 가족이 주방에서 먹고 있었어요.

## Active Writing

1. **Prompt:** A Busy Weekend: Describe what two different people in your house were doing yesterday.
   **Corrected Answer:** 어제 욕실을 청소하고 있었어요. (I was cleaning the bathroom.) / 어제 욕실에서 씻고 있었어요. (I was washing up in the bathroom.)

2. **Prompt:** The Dream House: Describe a "dream" scene in a house right now.
   **Corrected Answer:** 큰 거실에서 쉬고 있어요. 그리고 제 고양이가 가구 위에서 자고 있어요.

3. **Prompt:** The Cooking Disaster: Describe a scene in the kitchen.
   **Corrected Answer:** 가족이 주방에서 요리하고 있어요. 내 동생이 고기를 굽고 있고, 저는 채소를 씻고 있어요.
