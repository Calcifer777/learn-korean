생일 Birthday

한국에서는 생일에 미역국을 먹어요.
In Korea, people eat seaweed soup on their birthdays.

미역은 피를 맑게 해 주고 상처를 낫게 해 줘요.
Seaweed cleans the blood and helps heal wounds.

그래서 옛날부터 엄마들은 아기를 낳은 후에 미역국을 먹었어요.
So, since long ago, mothers have eaten seaweed soup after giving birth to a baby.

그러면 생일에는 왜 미역국을 먹을까요?
Then, why do we eat seaweed soup on our birthdays?

엄마가 나를 힘들게 낳아 줬잖아요.
It's because our mothers went through a hard time giving birth to us.

그래서 자신이 태어난 생일에 낳아 준 엄마에게 감사하는 마음에서 먹기 시작했다고 해요.
So, it is said that people started eating it on their birthdays to show gratitude to the mother who gave birth to them.
