회식

저희 회사는 회식을 자주 해요.
Our company has team dinners often.

어제도 회식을 했어요.
We had a team dinner yesterday, too.

어제는 저희 팀 사람들끼리만 회식을 했어요.
Yesterday, only the people on our team had a team dinner.

1년에 한 번 정도 전체 회식을 해요.
About once a year, we have a company-wide dinner.

그때는 회사의 모든 사람들이 모여서 회식을 해요.
At that time, everyone in the company gets together for a dinner.

팀 사람들끼리는 자주 회식을 해요.
Between team members, we have dinners often.

주로 저녁을 먹으면서 간단하게 술을 마셔요.
We usually drink lightly while eating dinner.

가끔 술을 아주 많이 마실 때도 있어요.
Sometimes, we drink a lot of alcohol.

---

회사, Company
회식, Company dinner
자주, Often
어제, Yesterday
사람들, People
끼리, Among/Between (each other)
1년, One year
정도, About/Approximately
전체, Whole/Entire
모든, All/Every
모이다, To gather/get together
저녁, Dinner/Evening
먹다, To eat
간단하다, To be simple/light
술을 마시다, To drink alcohol
아주, Very
많이, A lot/Many
