 My beginner level grammar learning modules include: 1. Present & Past Tense, Basic verb conjugation for current and finished actions. 2. Present Perfect & Past, Differences in time-related sentence endings. 3. Future Tense, Expressing what will happen or what I plan to do. 4. Modals: Want to..., Have to..., May/Might <do something>, Can/Could <do something>, Should <do something>. 5. Conditionals: If..., Using -면 and -다면 for hypothetical situations. 6. The Passive Voice, Understanding how actions are received by subjects. 7. Reported Speech, How to quote others and relay information. 8. Questions & Auxiliaries, Formatting questions and using helper verbs. Advanced Structures, """Question word + to,"" ""Used to,"" ""About ...ing.""" 9. Determiners, "Words that specify nouns (this, that, which)." 10. Relative & What Clauses, Linking sentences and creating complex descriptions. 11. Adjectives & Adverbs, Modifying verbs and nouns to add detail and color.
